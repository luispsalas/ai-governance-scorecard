        Glossary

        Cross-cutting terms used across the metrics above.

        RAG
        Retrieval-Augmented Generation — fetch relevant documents, then condition the model's answer on them.
        Chunk
        A passage the source corpus is split into for embedding and retrieval. Chunk size strongly affects precision/recall.
        Reranker
        A second-stage model that reorders retrieved chunks by relevance before they reach the generator.
        Embedding similarity
        Cosine distance between vector representations of text — a proxy for semantic closeness.
        MRR
        Mean Reciprocal Rank — averages 1/(rank of first relevant result); rewards putting the right answer first.
        NDCG
        Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain — ranking quality that accounts for graded relevance and position.
        LLM-as-judge
        Using a strong model to score outputs (faithfulness, relevance) where exact-match scoring doesn't apply.
        P50 / P95 / P99
        Latency percentiles — half / 95% / 99% of requests finish at or below this time. Tails matter more than the mean.
        PSI
        Population Stability Index — measures how much a distribution has shifted; >0.2 typically signals meaningful drift.
        KL divergence
        A statistical measure of how one probability distribution differs from a reference — used for drift detection.
        PII
        Personally Identifiable Information — names, IDs, emails, etc. that privacy law (GDPR/CCPA) protects.
        DLP
        Data Loss Prevention — controls that detect and protect sensitive data from leaving governed boundaries.
        HITL
        Human-in-the-Loop — a person reviews, approves or overrides AI decisions before they take effect.
        Drift
        Gradual change in input data (data drift) or model predictions (model drift) that degrades performance over time.
        TTFT
        Time To First Token — how long until streaming output begins; drives perceived responsiveness.
